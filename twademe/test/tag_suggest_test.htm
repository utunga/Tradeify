(add tags, to find your message by)

When you add a tag to your message e.g. '#herbs' it makes it easy for people who are looking for herbs to find your message. To add a tag to your message you write a word after a hash - #like_this, or just click on the suggested tags. Tags cannot contain spaces.

Tags are like categories, except *you* get to make up the categories.
