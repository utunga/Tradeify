Get started!

The best way to get going with the Growers Market is to post some of your own messages.

Try to think of all the things you might be able to offer. Or what might you want?

Here are some suggestions to get you thinking..

Do you have any extra fruit, veges, leafy greens or herbs in your garden?

How about honey, milk, nuts, grains, chickens or prepared food like jams or soups?

Do you have any seeds, seedlings, trees, or garden supplies to offer? Worm tea?

Or can you offer gardening services?

Advice, planning or labour?

If not that, how about food growing and seasonal recipe books and magazines?

Or could you provide food growing workshops?

And what about transport for that load of horse manure?

If you can't offer any of those things, maybe you want some.

Don't be shy - if you speak up to say what you need and where, someone in your community might just come forward and offer it.

Whatever you do - have fun

When you add a tag to your message e.g. '#herbs' it makes it easy for people who are looking for herbs to find your message.

To add a tag to your message click on one of the suggested tags, or add your own.

Tags are like categories, only you can have more than one tag per message, and you get to pick the tags.

The 'until date' is simply the date that you are making your offer or want available until. Eg. If you have plums for offer, you already know that they will rot within a few days so you can choose a date a few days hence. The until date is optional.

This is where you indicate what method you are willing to trade with.

For instance, if you just want to give your offer away, then click free. If you would be open to swap for something else, then click barter.

If you only want cash then click cash. If you're happy with either barter or cash, then click both barter and cash.

Simply write your suburb in the location box eg. "Titirangi". Check that the map shows the correct location, and include more information if it does not eg. "Titirangi, NZ".

If you wish, you may include your exact address, but we ask only for your general locality or town at this time.

Here you can preview and edit your message one more time before it gets posted.

You can edit the message any way you like provided you include a location and a type of transaction tag (#free, #barter or #cash).

Also the '@ooooby' tag is required at the start of the message. That way, if you have twitter, you can post a message formatted like this and have it show up in the marketplace.

This form helps you construct a message to post to the marketplace.

Something like:

I am offering what you are offering in your locality or town for #free, #barter or #cash until an end date (optional)

On this page you see all 'offers' or 'wanted messages' other people from Ooooby have made

Click a persons name to go to their profile page where you can post a message on their wall asking them about the offer. (If you are already 'friends' you will be able to message them directly. Otherwise, perhaps just leave your phone number in your message?)

Messages are listed by date on the list tab, but click on the tags to drill down into more specific areas or locations.

On the map tab click the red badges to see offers tagged for that area. Wanted messages are in blue.
