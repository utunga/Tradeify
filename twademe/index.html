For Trades In Person ( why? )
You are free to do what you like, but we recommend that you complete your trades in person. We believe in our community, and we known that most people are good - but - caveat emptor - buyer beware. That means its up to you to make sure things go smoothly - and one of the best ways to do that is to meet people in person. Twade me is all about the free and simple,but a part of that means its on you to make it work.Pay attention to people's reputation scores and have a good look at their web page (if they have one). And finally, if you get scammed, please let other's know via the reputation feedback, and we'll be sorry to hear it, but we cannot be held liable.
